# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two audiences on one marketplace:
- **Students / job-seekers (primary on landing):** applying into a void on Naukri/LinkedIn/Indeed, never hearing back. Context: deciding whether unGhost is worth signing up for. Job to be done: get real replies + prove skills.
- **Recruiters:** drowning in unqualified applicants. Job to be done: hire pre-qualified, AI-ranked candidates and commit to a response SLA.

## Product Purpose

India-first hiring platform built around accountability. Every recruiter commits to a public response SLA (24/48/72h); miss it and the student's application credit is refunded. AI grades application fit + integrity. Bootcamps award Verified Skill badges recruiters can filter on. Success = a student trusts that applying here actually gets a reply, and signs up.

## Brand Personality

Direct, honest, slightly defiant. Voice: plain and specific, never buzzword marketing. Three words: accountable, sharp, no-nonsense. Emotional goal: relief + trust ("finally, someone answers").

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-SaaS landing: purple gradient mesh, three equal feature cards, fake metric hero, eyebrow above every section.
- Fake social proof: real company logos/names the platform doesn't actually have; invented precise stats (92% match, 75% stat) presented as fact.
- Job-board clutter (Naukri/Indeed volume-optimised noise) — the thing we position against.

## Design Principles

1. **Show the mechanic, not a made-up number.** The SLA-or-refund loop is the proof; no invented statistics.
2. **No fake data.** Illustrative demos are labeled; real DB data or nothing.
3. **One promise, both sides.** Symmetry (no-ghost either direction) is the through-line.
4. **Honest restraint.** Motion and color serve comprehension; brand-blue is the single committed accent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on body + buttons. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` support (motion already gated). Keyboard-reachable CTAs.
